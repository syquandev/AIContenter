Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2025

1 Introduction

AIContenter ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

2.1 Information from Facebook Login

When you log in through Facebook, we receive:

Data Type Purpose Stored?
Name & Profile Picture Display in app interface Session only
Email Address Account identification Session only
Facebook Pages List Page management features Session only
Page Access Tokens Publishing & analytics Encrypted, local device
Page Engagement Data Analytics dashboard Session only

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App usage data and interaction patterns (anonymized)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

2.3 Content You Create

  • Post drafts and AI-generated content (stored locally on your device)
  • Scheduling preferences and publishing history
  • Media files selected for posts (processed locally, not uploaded to our servers)

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide core app functionality including content creation, scheduling, and publishing
  • AI Content Generation: To generate personalized content suggestions based on your topics and preferences
  • Analytics: To display engagement metrics for your Facebook Pages
  • App Improvement: To analyze anonymized usage patterns and improve app performance
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

🛡️ We do NOT: Sell your personal data to third parties, use your content for advertising, or share your Facebook credentials with anyone.

4 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Facebook/Meta: We interact with Facebook's Graph API to provide our services. Your data is subject to Facebook's Data Policy when transmitted through their API.
  • AI Service Providers: Content generation requests are processed through AI providers. Only your topic description is transmitted — no personal data or Facebook credentials.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties for their commercial purposes.

5 Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain
  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.2+)
  • Post drafts and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain centralized databases of user content
  • Access tokens are automatically invalidated upon logout

6 Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Session Data: Cleared when you log out of the App
  • Local Storage: Removed when you delete the App from your device
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized data may be retained for up to 12 months for service improvement
  • Account Deletion: Upon request, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days

7 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data and account
  • Revocation: Revoke Facebook permissions at any time through your Facebook Settings
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of analytics data collection through App settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@aicontenter.app or use the Account Deletion feature within the App's Settings.

8 Children's Privacy

AIContenter is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete such data.

9 International Data Transfers

If you access the App from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in a different country. By using the App, you consent to such transfers, provided that appropriate safeguards are in place.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11 Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: support@aicontenter.app

Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
